ABOUT US



StockX is the Stock Market Of Things – A live ‘Bid/Ask’ marketplace. Buyers place bids, sellers place asks & when a bid & ask meet, the transaction happens StockX, the stock market for sneakers, is an entirely new way of buying and selling sneakers. Our live marketplace means that you no longer have to scour the entire internet to find the best price on the pair you want. We bring it all to you, in one place. StockX also lets you sell your pairs for a fair market value without having to negotiate with every single potential buyer, one by one.
